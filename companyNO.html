about us

Inova is a newly formed company which loves innovation. We believe that everyone can innovate! But to be a good innovator you have to practice, that is why we decided to make courses for you. We are hosting courses for mentoring and prototyping in Oslo. Everyone can join our prototyping course, this course is also suitable for young adults. The mentor course is planned for more experienced innovators.

We are ambitious and have a lot planned for the future. We are planning to make some courses especially for the young innovators, which parents also can join. In the future we want to host even more courses in different cities in Norway.

Once a year we are hosting team competitions in Oslo, Ski, Bergen and Kristiansand.
